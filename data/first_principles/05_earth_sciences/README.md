# 05 — Earth Sciences

> *"The present is the key to the past."* — Charles Lyell (Principle of Uniformitarianism)

## Scope

Earth Sciences study the physical and chemical constitution of the Earth and its atmosphere. This domain applies principles from physics, chemistry, and biology to understand planetary-scale processes — from tectonic plate movements to atmospheric dynamics to ocean circulation.

## Branches Covered

| Branch | Directory | Focus |
|--------|-----------|-------|
| **Geology** | `geology/` | Uniformitarianism, plate tectonics, stratigraphy, mineral physics |
| **Geophysics** | `geophysics/` | Seismic wave propagation, gravity, Earth's magnetic field |
| **Meteorology & Climatology** | `meteorology_and_climatology/` | Atmospheric thermodynamics, radiative transfer, climate systems |
| **Oceanography** | `oceanography/` | Ocean circulation, thermohaline dynamics, marine chemistry |
| **Paleontology** | `paleontology/` | Fossil record, biostratigraphy, mass extinction principles |
| **Environmental Science** | `environmental_science/` | Biogeochemical cycles, ecosystem services, sustainability principles |

## Research Approach

1. Start with **Geology** — uniformitarianism and plate tectonics as foundational principles
2. **Geophysics** — application of physics to Earth systems
3. **Meteorology** — atmospheric science principles
4. **Oceanography** and **Paleontology**
5. **Environmental Science** — integrative principles
6. Distinguish principles *unique to Earth science* from *applications of physics/chemistry*

## Key Questions

- Is uniformitarianism a first principle or a methodological assumption?
- Are there Earth-specific laws beyond applied physics and chemistry?
- What is the status of plate tectonics — a theory, a law, or a framework?
- How do Earth sciences handle the tension between determinism and historical contingency?

## Dependencies

- **Requires:** Physics (mechanics, thermodynamics, fluid dynamics), Chemistry, Biology (for paleontology, ecology)
- **Required by:** Environmental Science, Climate Science

## Phase

**Phase 5** — After physics, chemistry, and biology foundations.
